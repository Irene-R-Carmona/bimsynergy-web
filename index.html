What is BIMSynergy?

BIMSynergy is a digital platform that merges Building Information Modeling (BIM) with digital twins, IoT, and AI. It allows you to visualize, monitor, and optimize your building‚Äôs performance in real time, from design and construction to daily operations and maintenance.

  • Connects BIM models (Revit, ArchiCAD, etc.) with live IoT data
  • Provides real-time dashboards for energy, occupancy, and climate
  • Offers AI-powered analytics and predictive maintenance
  • Automates sustainability tracking and compliance reporting
  • Facilitates collaboration between all project stakeholders

Key Features

Digital Twin Engine

Transforms BIM models into interactive, real-time digital twins. Visualize your building‚Äôs status, assets, and performance at a glance.

IoT Integration Hub

Connects seamlessly with building sensors and systems. Collects and displays live data on energy, temperature, occupancy, air quality, and more.

AI-Powered Analytics

Uses machine learning to detect patterns, predict maintenance needs, and suggest optimizations for energy and resource use.

Sustainability Dashboard

Tracks carbon footprint, water usage, and other sustainability metrics. Supports compliance with LEED, BREEAM, and local codes.

Collaborative Workflows

Enables seamless communication and task management for architects, engineers, facility managers, and building owners.

Automated Compliance

Checks your designs and operations against building codes and sustainability standards, providing instant feedback and reporting.
